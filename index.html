How to make cheesecakes?

To make a classic cheesecake, start by mixing 1 1/2 cups of graham cracker crumbs, 2 tablespoons of sugar, and 1/2 cup of melted unsalted butter for the crust, pressing it into the bottom of a springform pan. For the filling, beat 4 packages of softened cream cheese with 1 cup of sugar and 1 teaspoon of vanilla extract until smooth. Add 4 eggs one at a time, then mix in 2/3 cup of sour cream. Pour the filling over the crust, bake in a preheated oven at 325°F (163°C) for about 55 minutes, and chill before serving.
